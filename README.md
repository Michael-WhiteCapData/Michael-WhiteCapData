### 🧩 What I do

Most companies don't need another dashboard — they need their CRM, POS, email, and spreadsheets to share **one source of truth**. That integration work is the core of what I do at **WhiteCap Data**, and everything else builds on it:

- 🔗 **Data integration & connective tissue** — stitching disparate vendor tools (CRM, POS, billing, comms) into reliable, automated pipelines instead of manual copy-paste.
- 🤖 **AI & agent engineering** — production LLM agents and automation: Claude / MCP tooling, voice agents (Pipecat STT→LLM→TTS), and lead-gen pipelines that do real work.
- 🛠️ **Infrastructure & DevOps** — a self-hosted **5-node Kubernetes (k3s) cluster**, OPNsense networking with split-tunnel VPN, Tailscale mesh, monitoring, and hardened Linux servers.
- 🌐 **Full-stack & web** — Cloudflare-native services, web-scraping → lead-gen → shippable sites, and multi-tenant SaaS for B2B clients.
